## Mission

Build an outlier trading platform for independent retail traders that helps them
trade smarter than standard retail workflows by combining:

- institutional flow awareness
- institutional inefficiency exploitation
- AI-assisted decision support
- market-structure-aware execution guidance

## Positioning

This product is built for retail traders, not institutions.

We aim to:

- move with institutional strength when flow and structure confirm it
- attack institutional constraints and inefficiencies when edge appears
- use small-account agility (speed, flexibility, position sizing precision)

We do not aim to be:

- a copy of institutional terminals
- a generic scanner dashboard
- a one-style ideology (always trend or always fade)

## Product Promise

The platform must deliver:

- high-quality signals with clear rationale
- execution and risk guidance that is practical in live markets
- transparent context (flow, structure, volatility, catalyst state)
- measurable improvement in trader decision quality over time

## Strategy Stack (Role-Based)

- `VOYAGER`: align with durable institutional accumulation and leadership
- `SNIPER`: capture tactical momentum and breakout timing inefficiencies
- `REMORA`: capture short-horizon stealth institutional accumulation / quiet flow
- `SHORT`: capture deterioration before full repricing is complete
- `CONTRARIAN`: exploit panic, forced selling, and volatility overshoots

See `STRATEGY_DOCTRINE.md` for the operating mandate, edge claim, failure mode,
and readiness standard for each sleeve.

## Boundaries And Responsibility

- We are a retail trading intelligence and execution-assist platform.
- We are not a fund, broker-dealer, or investment advisor.
- We provide signals, context, and risk frameworks.
- End users choose their own trades and remain responsible for outcomes.
- We do not promise profits or guaranteed performance.

## Decision Filter

When priorities conflict, choose the path that best answers "yes" to these:

1. Does this increase controlled account-growth potential?
2. Does this improve alignment with institutional strength when appropriate?
3. Does this improve exploitation of institutional inefficiency when appropriate?
4. Does this improve explainability, selectivity, and measurable edge?
5. Does this make retail traders meaningfully more capable?

If the answer is mostly "no", it is off-mission.

## Design Constraint

Do not drift toward a generic "institutional platform" identity.

The correct target is:

- institutional discipline
- retail usability
- retail agility
- explicit exploitation of institutional inefficiency where edge exists

If a feature adds enterprise-style complexity without improving retail edge, it
is the wrong feature.
